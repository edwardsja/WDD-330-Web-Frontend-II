      Hints:

      Hint 1: There are 4 pairs of opposite elements. The single element dragons of opposite types cannot directly breed with each other.
      Example: A "Fire" and "Ice" dragon cannot breed. But, an "Inferno" (Fire, Wind) and an "Ice" dragon can breed

        Opposite Element List
      • Fire, Ice
      • Rock, Air
      • Grass, Metal
      • Water, Lightning

      Hint 2: To get more rare dragons, try breeding two dragons of the same rarity. Breeding certain dragons of the same rarity can result in higher rarity.
      Example: Breeding a specific Uncommon dragon with an specific Uncommon dragon can sometimes yield a Rare dragon

      Hint 3: There are 80 total dragons.

      • 14 Common Dragons
      • 22 Uncommon Dragons
      • 24 Rare Dragons
      • 8 Very Rare Dragons
      • 8 Epic Dragons
      • 4 Legendary Dragons
